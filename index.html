Improving Video Virtual Try-On with Garment-aware Spatio-Temporal Attention

Yongkang Wang1, Dong Li2, Wei Yu2, Yingwei Pan2, Lei Zhao1, Ting Yao2, Tao Mei2,
1Zhejiang University
2HiDream.ai Inc

Abstract

Video virtual try-on aims to realistically outfit a subject in a video with a specific garment. The primary challenge is to maintain the garment's visual integrity while dynamically adjusting to the subject's movements. Existing methods typically employ a two-stage process that separately handles appearance and motion, often leading to appearance degradation and temporal inconsistencies. To address this, we propose Gar-VVTO, a one-stage end-to-end framework based on Stable Video Diffusion. Gar-VVTO innovatively introduces a Garment-aware Spatio-Temporal Attention (GSTA) module to preserve the authenticity of the garment while ensuring spatio-temporal consistency. GSTA leverages a learnable garment-agnostic spatio-temporal latents bank to capture common garment characteristics from the training videos, such as topological structures and motion patterns, thereby maintaining crucial structural details. Additionally, GSTA integrates garment appearance features to complement the garment-specific texture details. Moreover, Gar-VVTO capitalizes on a temporal variation alignment loss between consecutive frames to enhance temporal coherence. Extensive experiments conducted on VVT and ViViD datasets demonstrate the superiority of our Gar-VVTO against the state-of-the-art methods.
